Location: Vatican

Geographical Location: Vatican City

The Vatican, home to St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel, is a hub of religious and artistic treasures, showcasing Renaissance masterpieces and the iconic Michelangelo's frescoes.

Situated in Rome, Italy, the Vatican City is the smallest independent state globally, surrounded by Rome's historic city walls, offering a blend of religious significance and architectural marvels.

Location: Big Ben

Geographical Location: London, England

Big Ben, part of the Palace of Westminster, is an iconic symbol of London, known for its majestic clock tower and the melodious chimes that resonate across the River Thames.

Located in London, England, the city embraces a rich history and cultural diversity, with landmarks like Buckingham Palace and the British Museum, making it a vibrant metropolis.

Location: Taj Mahal

Geographical Location: Agra, India

The Taj Mahal, a marvel of Mughal architecture, stands as a testament to eternal love, with its white marble façade and intricate carvings, creating a breathtaking spectacle.

Nestled in Agra, India, the Taj Mahal overlooks the Yamuna River and is surrounded by lush gardens, offering a serene ambiance in the heart of historical India.

Location: Mount Fuji

Geographical Location: Fujinomiya, Japan

Mount Fuji, Japan's highest peak, is an iconic stratovolcano with a symmetrical cone, attracting climbers and providing breathtaking views of cherry blossoms in spring.

Situated in Fujinomiya, Japan, Mount Fuji is part of the scenic Fuji-Hakone-Izu National Park, offering a picturesque landscape with lakes and hot springs.

Location: La Sagrada Familia

Geographical Location: Barcelona, Spain

La Sagrada Familia, designed by Antoni Gaudí, is a masterpiece of modernist architecture, featuring intricate sculptures and unique design elements that make it a symbol of Barcelona.

Located in Barcelona, Spain, the city boasts a lively culture, Mediterranean beaches, and architectural wonders, with Gaudí's influence evident in various landmarks like Park Güell.
